PatwardhanMed Consulting provides comprehensive regulatory and quality consulting services for medical device and pharmaceutical manufacturers worldwide.

Founded in February 2022 by Yasha Patwardhan, PatwardhanMed Consulting is dedicated to providing high-quality regulatory compliance and quality management system (QMS) consulting services for medical device and pharmaceutical manufacturers. Our approach begins with listening, learning, and thorough analysis to deliver tailored solutions. We recognize that there’s no one-size-fits-all method for reducing complexity and redundancy in Risk Management and QMS processes while ensuring ongoing regulatory compliance. With over 15 years of proven client satisfaction, our decision-making is focused on delivering sustainable, client-centered project outcome.

Our Philosophy


Client satisfaction is our top priority. However, the greatest value we bring as consultants lies in our extensive network of trusted professionals and affiliates. We ensure that our clients are never left "cleaning up" after another consultant's work, as poorly implemented changes and executed projects can damage both professional trust and our client's long-term success. Our approach is straightforward: 'If we would not cook it in our kitchen, we won't recommend it to anyone else. Ever'.

We understand that medical device regulations often leave room for interpretation, and implementing effective quality management systems (QMS) can be complex. At PatwardhanMed Consulting, we specialize in simplifying this process, ensuring your systems meet regulatory standards while remaining efficient, scalable, and tailored to your specific needs. Our insights help ensure not only immediate success but also long-term sustainability of outcomes.
